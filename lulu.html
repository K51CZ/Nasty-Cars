Why Called Nasty


In this site,we mainly focus on Super cars.The streets are crawling with souped-up nightmares, each more absurd than the last. There’s the Mclaren, dripping with neon decals and a sound system that rattles windows before you even hear the engine roar. Next, a Bugatti chiron with a garish matte-black wrap, oversized rims, and exhaust tips that look more like rocket launchers than anything you’d find on a car. Don’t forget the Supra with a garish hood scoop, the kind that makes you question if it's a car or a mobile wind tunnel. Then there's the Dodge Charger blaring bass so loud it practically shakes the air, sporting chrome fenders and a suspension so low it scrapes at every bump.It's all a symphony of bad taste and raw power, a cacophony of loud engines and even louder egos.
